## What is Play Harmonium?

Play Harmonium is a browser-based harmonium practice project with a tool-first landing page and a lightweight SaaS backbone.

The current product strategy is simple:

- let users play immediately on the homepage or focused keyboard page
- support keyboard shortcuts, touch input, Sargam labels, octave switching, and transpose
- validate search demand with useful blog content before building heavier premium features

## What the Current Build Focuses On

The current version is optimized for fast validation:

- a tool-first landing page
- a dedicated `/keyboard` practice route
- starter SEO content and update logs
- reusable auth, payments, analytics, and admin infrastructure for later expansion

## What May Come Later

If the keyword shows durable demand, the next layers can include:

- real harmonium sample packs
- saved presets and session history
- recording and playback
- MIDI support
- premium practice packs
