## Overview

Play Harmonium is a browser-based music practice website. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you use the site.

## Information We May Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

1. **Account Information**
   - If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication details needed to manage access.

2. **Usage Data**
   - We may collect information about pages visited, device type, browser type, interaction events, and general feature usage so we can improve the product.

3. **Payment Data**
   - If paid plans are enabled, payment details are processed by third-party payment providers. We do not intentionally store full card details on our own servers.

4. **Support Messages**
   - If you contact us, we may keep the information you send so we can respond and improve support quality.

## How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

- operate and secure the service
- improve performance and usability
- analyze feature adoption and search traffic quality
- process payments and manage subscriptions
- respond to support requests

## Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, and analytics tools to remember preferences, understand traffic sources, and improve product decisions. You can limit cookies in your browser settings, though some features may work less effectively.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the site, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, email, and payment platforms.

We may also disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect our rights, users, or service integrity.

## Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for legitimate business, legal, operational, or security purposes.
